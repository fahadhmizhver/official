About

Syllables wrought in the crucible of hush, their quintessence tempered by an eternal vigil.

Mohammed Fahadh Khambrath, known professionally as Fahadh MizhVer, is an Indian English-language author recognized for his work in the psychological thriller and dark romance genres.

Born on May 31, 2008, in Kerala, India, MizhVer published his debut book, an ancient fiction love story titled Eleanor of the Whitecliff Manor from the series The Aphrodite Amour-Writs, at the age of seventeen. He writes in the crime and psychological thriller genres, as well as love and dark romance.

Fahadh MizhVer also founded The Khambrath Group & Co., an international trade intermediary specializing in geopolitical arbitrage.
