MIZVER (Mohammed Fahadh Khambrath; also styled as Fahadh MizhVer) is an Indian writer of classical history and historically inspired fiction. His work often centers on the social, cultural, and lived experiences of ancient Rome and the wider Greco-Roman world.

Born on May 31, 2008, in Kerala, India, he published his debut book, an ancient-fiction love story titled Korvian from the series The Aphrodite Amour-Writs, at the age of seventeen.

In addition to historical romance, his writing spans crime and psychological thriller fiction, frequently emphasizing character psychology, intimacy, and the pressures of power.

He is also associated with The Khambrath Group & Co., which is described in his public profile as an international trade intermediary.

Early life and background

MIZVER was born in Kerala, India. His public author biography describes an early interest in classical history and the everyday realities of antiquity, how people loved, feared, negotiated status, and made meaning under the structures of empire.

Rather than treating the ancient world only as spectacle, he frames it as a lived environment defined by family, labor, politics, belief, and private desire.

Writing

MIZVER’s fiction ranges from historically inspired romance to crime and psychological thrillers. Across genres, his work foregrounds interiority and moral ambiguity, focusing on what characters conceal as much as what they say.

His historical narratives are commonly anchored in social detail, customs, public life, and the private economies of affection, using the past as a lens for questions of identity, intimacy, and power.

Entrepreneurship

Outside writing, he founded The Khambrath Group & Co., described as an international trade intermediary focused on geopolitical arbitrage.

Personal life

Public biographical notes describe MIZVER as balancing writing with entrepreneurial work. He has spoken of an interest in the private side of history, domestic life, duty, and the way relationships are shaped by status and circumstance, which also informs his fiction.

He is based in Kerala, India, and is known online by his pen name MIZVER.

Family

MIZVER is the son of Mohammed Haneefa Khambrath and Sajna. He has one brother, Muhammed Ramzan Khambrath (also known as RAAZ MIZVER), and two sisters, Shahadiya (elder) and Farisha.
